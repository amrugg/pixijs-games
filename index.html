My name is Aaron Rugg. I can program web games for you if you want. I have made a few games before as presents to my relatives. I prefer to use Pixijs to program games, but I can use HTML and CSS too. One big project that I made was a ship sailing game where you fight pirates. You can move with the wind or with your oars. There are islands you can sail around (but not into, or you crash). You fire cannonballs at pirates, who drop barrels of loot you can use to repair your ship or get more cannonballs. Your goal is to destroy 5 frigates and take the treasure. Thank you for reading.
